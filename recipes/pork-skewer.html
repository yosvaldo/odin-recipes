Asian Pork Skewer

This is Asian Pork Skewer. It is using few asian seasoning notably soy sauce. After being chopped it skewered and grilled

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 800 grams Pork
  • 200 grams Lard

Spices

  • 9 pieces Shallots
  • 5 pieces Garlics
  • 2 pieces Chilis
  • 5 pieces Candlenuts
  • 2 pieces Galangals
  • 1 sachet Cilantros
  • 1 piece Ginger (about 2cm)
  • 1 piece Turmeric (about 2cm)

Additional Spices

  • 70 grams of Brown Sugar
  • 5 tablespoons of Sweet Soy Sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of Salty Soy Sauce
  • 10 pieces of Lime Leaves
  • Butter to taste

Steps

  1. Cut off the Pork and Lard
  2. Use blender to mix the Spices and add 5 pieces of Lime Leaf
  3. Fry the mixed Spices with little amount of Butter
  4. Add Brown Sugar, Sweet Soy Sauce, and Salty Soy Sauce to it, keep frying until cooked
  5. Mix it with the Pork, Lard, and remaining Lime Leaf
  6. Skewer the Pork and Lard then store it at fridge for at least 2 hourse
  7. Grill it. Once it half cooked, apply the remaining Additional Spices to it
  8. Once it cooked, it's ready to eat. You can add Sweet Soy Sauce, chopped Chili, chopped Shallot, and chopped Lime Leaf for dipping sauce
